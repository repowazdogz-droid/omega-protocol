Omega Spatial Gallery

Static, inspectable spatial templates for reasoning. No simulation. No autonomy. No recommendations.

Trust contract: Human-led · Non-autonomous · Visual reasoning only · Uncertainty is shown, not hidden · If it feels like a simulator or decision engine, it has failed.
V1
Trust Surface (Robot Workspace)

Layers + inspection in a calm static scene. Proves the interaction contract.
T2
Causal Map

Influence links + disallowed links + uncertainty halos. No forecasting.
T3
Assumption Surface

Assumptions, conflicts, unsupported regions. Finds why teams disagree.
T4
Tradeoff Atlas

Objectives + constraints + feasible region + tradeoff front. No "best choice".
T5
Assurance Ladder

Inputs → authority → outcomes, constrained by overrides. Not procedures, not certification.
